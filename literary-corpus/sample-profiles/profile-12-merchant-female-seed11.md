> [!background] Background
> **Age 18 (709 TR)** — She watched the web of favours and enmities carefully, learning where to place her feet.
> **Age 19 (710 TR)** — A woman she knew was killed — a neighbour, a trader she dealt with, someone whose face she knew. It was the kind of death that made everyone quieter for a while.
> **Age 20 (711 TR)** — She entered her husband's household. The first months were an exercise in understanding the rules of a different world. Married Trovan Brightcoin (merchant, age 24)
> **Age 20 (711 TR)** — She walked a long way with someone she loved without talking much. The quiet was good. She held onto the memory of it.
> **Age 21 (712 TR)** — Another difficult year. Life continued.
> **Age 21 (712 TR)** — Her husband was ill for months. She nursed him, managed the household, and kept the worry mostly to herself. He recovered. She had not been sure he would.
> **Age 22 (713 TR)** — She watched the mistress take apart a piece she had thought was finished and rebuild the central section from scratch in forty minutes. It was the same result, technically — the dimensions matched, the specifications were met. But the new version had something the first version had not had, something she could see when they were side by side but cannot yet name precisely. The mistress saw her looking. She did not explain. She said: when you can say what the difference is, you are ready for the examination.
> **Age 22 (713 TR)** — She sat up all night with someone sick. Nothing dramatic happened. By dawn they were through the worst and she went about her day.
> **Age 23 (714 TR)** — The fever broke on the fourth day. The Esolani who had been attending her — a woman her own age, mild-faced, entirely competent — told her she was past the worst and brought broth. She had not expected to survive. She had a different relationship with the Peonian church after that.
