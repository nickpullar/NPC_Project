> [!background] Background
> **Age 18 (717 TR)** — She kept the household alive through ingenuity and stubbornness.
> **Age 19 (718 TR)** — The recovery was long. She measured it in small returns — the walk she could finish, the task she could complete.
> **Age 20 (719 TR)** — She wrote off four debts this campaign. Two dead, one deserted, one simply gone. She adjusts the prices for everyone else accordingly, which is the honest way to run a sutler's operation: the cost of unrecoverable debt is distributed across the credit that does get repaid. She does not complain about this. It is a known cost of the trade. She does note the names carefully, because some of the deserters end up in other armies, and the road network is smaller than it appears.
> **Age 21 (720 TR)** — She could tell rain from cloud before most felt the wind shift.
