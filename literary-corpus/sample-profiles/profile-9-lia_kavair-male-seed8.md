> [!background] Background
> **Age 18 (712 TR)** — Necessity made him the nearest thing to a healer in his community.
> **Age 19 (713 TR)** — He made a habit of it — the early mornings, the regular exertion. It accumulated.
> **Age 20 (714 TR)** — The master struck him across the ear for a piece of work that was not good enough. Not a severe beating — a correction, delivered with the flat of the hand and the specific economy of someone for whom this was a routine instructional tool rather than an expression of anger. The work had not been good enough. He accepted this. He also noted, privately, the specific quality of the master's expression: not angry, not sadistic, but impatient in the way of someone who expected better and was expressing that expectation physically because the verbal expression had not been sufficient. He made the work better. The master did not comment on the improvement.
> **Age 21 (715 TR)** — A killing in the neighbourhood pulled him in — as witness, as kin, as someone who knew both sides. He had to choose where he stood, or at least appear to. Feud with Roric Edsen
> **Age 22 (716 TR)** — A gamble on goods paid off handsomely.
> **Age 23 (717 TR)** — He travelled far with a caravan, seeing parts of Kaldor few of his neighbours ever would.
> **Age 24 (718 TR)** — He enters every room the same way: two paces in, stop, count the doors and windows. It takes a heartbeat. He has done it so long he no longer thinks about it.
> **Age 25 (719 TR)** — Hunger, debt, or simple greed — he took what was not his.
> **Age 26 (720 TR)** — The job was handed to him without explanation — a merchant's strongbox, a specific night, a contact at the eastern gate. He did not know until afterward, when his Shadow named what he had become. There was no ceremony. There were only consequences.
