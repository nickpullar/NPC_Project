> **Age 18 (677 TR)** — She struck up an acquaintance that would prove valuable in years to come. Made contact: Dera Packworth (merchant)
> **Age 19 (678 TR)** — She was certain of the weather and entirely mistaken. She said nothing about it. Others said enough.
> **Age 20 (679 TR)** — She heard the cry and had the choice that women always have in these moments: whether joining would help or whether she would be more useful as a witness, which is a calculation she makes in approximately half a second. She positioned herself where she could see the man being pursued, watched where he went, and when the officers arrived she described his route and his description clearly and they found him two streets over. She was thanked by the bailiff. She has been a useful witness in two similar situations. She considers this a form of civic participation she is good at.
> **Age 21 (680 TR)** — The camp economy runs on the sutlers and everyone knows it, including the officers who would prefer not to acknowledge it. She extends credit carefully — who is reliable, who has family money, who is the kind of man who runs when things go wrong. She has been cheated precisely twice and recovered from both by knowing which sergeant to speak to. She is carrying the wages of four dead men in her ledger, which she will attempt to recover from their estates when the campaign ends, and which she is fairly certain she will not recover.
> **Age 22 (681 TR)** — She was asked to deliver a package to a specific house in a town sixty miles out and note, on the way back, what she could observe about the movement of men and horses on the Tashal road. She delivered the package. She observed the movement. Her report contained three pieces of information her officer had not expected, which she knows because of how he reacted to those three pieces. She is aware she is useful in a specific way. She is deciding what to do with that awareness.
> **Age 23 (682 TR)** — She married. She had opinions about the match that were not the primary consideration in making it. Married Rhoric Oring (soldier, age 27)
> **Age 24 (683 TR)** — She was not trained for battlefield wounds. She was trained for illness, for fever, for childbirth. She adapted in the first campaign and has been adapting since. The military surgeons, when there are any, do not always know more than she does. Sometimes they know less. She does the work that needs doing and has stopped spending energy on the question of whether she is supposed to be the one doing it.
